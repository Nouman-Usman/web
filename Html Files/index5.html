Volunteer Community

Guidelines / How to Share Volunteer Work on Punjab Youth Portal

Objectives

The objective of this activity is to encourage and motivate the youth of Punjab to participate in volunteer work, build up a caring community, promote the positive values of self-fulfillment and enhance the sense of social belongings. Our country is facing numerous social, environmental, political and cultural issues, and we need volunteers to fill the gap among different strata of the society in general and to give appreciation to Young Volunteers in particular.

Mechanism

Volunteerism among Youth will be promoted through sharing of short, evidence based Video Documentaries of the volunteer work undertaken by individuals or group of individuals.

Key Areas of Volunteerism

  • Community Projects
  • Volunteering in Hospitals
  • Green Volunteering
  • Human Rights
  • Child Protection
  • Environment
  • Disaster Preparedness
  • Information Technology
  • Preventive Health
  • Gender Equality
  • Poverty Alleviation
  • Women Empowerment
  • Peace Building
  • Inter-faith Harmony
  • Protection against Drug Abuse
  • Any other relevant Social Area

Who qualifies as a Youth Volunteer?

The Youth Volunteer programme is looking for motivated and talented youth between the ages of 15 and 29.

Eligibility

A volunteer should:

  1. be a citizen of Pakistan and should fall in age bracket of 15 to 29 (Official age-group of Youth).
  2. have valid CNIC or Form-B Certificate &father’s / guardian’s CNIC in case of under 18 years.
  3. have valid proof of age / date of birth.
  4. be an individual or group of individuals and not drawing any remuneration for the social work.
  5. Volunteer work should have been undertaken in Punjab

Process / Steps

  • Register at Punjab Youth Portal (Click Here)
  • A Confirmation Email will be sent at your provided email address upon successful registration
  • Upload your prepared video (as per criteria given below) on YouTube or Dailymotion.
  • Login  (Click Here) to submit your entry and link to the uploaded video.
  • A Confirmation Email will be sent at your registered email address upon successful entry submission.

Note: Only qualified entries (as decided by the concerned committee) will be contacted within One Month of submission. Please do not call or email to inquire about the status of entry after submission.

Criteria for Videos Documentary

  1. Documentary must start with the title (area of volunteer work) and brief introduction of the volunteer (maximum length- 30 seconds).
  2. Documentary must have contents based on Social Volunteerism and its impact on the lives of people.
  3. Documentary should be based on evidence of volunteer work highlighting the work of volunteer.
  4. The submitted clips may preferably be 2-3 minutes long and it should not exceed 05 minutes. Submissions exceeding 05 minutes will be disqualified.
  5. Videos is to be uploaded on YouTube or Dailymotion as one single file in high quality.
  6. Short title of the entry and a descriptive text of maximum 1000 characters are to be entered in the online form at the time of Video submission.
  7. Documentary should be free of any gender, sectarian or religious discrimination.
  8. The documentary must be the original work of the volunteer.
  9. The footage/photos should be from Pakistan only.
  10. The video content must be in Urdu or English.
  11. Participants must only use royalty free music/original background score for the film to avoid any copyright issues.
